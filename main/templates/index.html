Who We Are

Cedossa is dedicated to transforming lives and creating opportunities for persons with Down Syndrome and Autism in Malawi.

Our Mission

To empower persons with Down Syndrome and Autism to thrive through advocacy, skills development, psychosocial counselling and trainings.

Our Vision

To ensure persons with Down Syndrome and Autism are accepted and enjoying their human rights like anyone else in Malawi.

Our Goals

  • Reduce infant mortality rate
  • Improve health and education services
  • Increase awareness and advocacy
  • Promote rights of people with Down Syndrome and Autism

Our Story

Founded in 2021, Cedossa has been at the forefront of championing the rights and welfare of persons with Down Syndrome and Autism in Malawi. What began as a small initiative has grown into a movement creating real change.

Through our programs and partnerships, we've impacted 1000+ lives and continue to expand our reach across the country.
