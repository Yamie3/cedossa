Down Syndrome Programs

At CEDOSSA, we provide specialized healthcare, educational support, and advocacy programs for children with Down Syndrome and their families across Malawi.

Support Our Programs

What We Offer

Healthcare Support

Regular check-ups, therapy sessions, and nutritional guidance tailored for children with Down Syndrome.

Education & Learning

Inclusive learning programs, developmental exercises, and individualized education plans to help children reach their potential.

Family & Community Support

Counseling, awareness programs, and advocacy to empower families and create inclusive communities.
