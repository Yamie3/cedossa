Autism Support Programs

CEDOSSA provides early intervention, sensory activities, speech therapy, and inclusive education programs to help children with autism thrive in their communities.

Support Our Programs

What We Offer

Early Intervention

Structured programs for early identification and therapy to support cognitive and social development.

Sensory & Speech Therapy

Targeted therapies to improve communication, sensory processing, and daily living skills.

Inclusive Education

Support for children to attend school and participate fully in learning alongside peers.
